Must-Visit Destinations

Belgium offers a diverse range of experiences. Here are some of our top picks for your Belgian adventure.

Brussels

The vibrant capital with stunning architecture, world-class museums, and the famous Grand Place.

Capital EU Hub Chocolate

Bruges

The fairy-tale medieval city with picturesque canals, cobbled streets, and romantic atmosphere.

Medieval Canals UNESCO

Ghent

A perfect blend of medieval charm and contemporary culture with stunning cathedrals and vibrant nightlife.

Historic Student City Art

Antwerp

The diamond capital with fashion, art, and a stunning cathedral in the heart of the Flemish region.

Diamonds Fashion Port City

Liège

The vibrant Walloon city with a rich history, famous waffles, and the impressive Montagne de Bueren.

Wallonia Waffles History

Ardennes

Belgium's natural paradise with dense forests, rolling hills, and outdoor activities for all seasons.

Nature Hiking Adventure
